Why We Started

Aama ko Maya Chhatrabas is a Hostel run by organization "Janjati kalyan Ashram, Nepal". It is non-governmental, not for profit,social welfare organization working among the indigenous tribes and the communities.

Preserving culture

Cultural Exchange Programs

We celebrate the richness of Janjati culture by organizing cultural exchange programs that allow for sharing traditions, rituals, art, and folklore with others. This promotes intercultural understanding and appreciation.

Traditional Arts and Crafts

The traditional arts and crafts of the Janjati community hold immense value. We encourage the preservation and promotion of these skills by providing platforms for local artisans to showcase their crafts and sell their products.

Cultural Festivals

Festivals are an integral part of cultural identity. We organize cultural festivals to revitalize and cherish the unique customs and traditions of the Janjati people, fostering a sense of pride and belonging.
