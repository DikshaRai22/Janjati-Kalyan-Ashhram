Protect The Children.

Janjati Kalyan Ashram is more than just a shelter; it is a place where children are welcomed into a warm and inclusive community. The dedicated staff and volunteers at Janjati Kalyan Ashram provide not only the basic necessities of life but also a sense of belonging, care, and guidance. The Ashram embraces cultural diversity, fostering an environment that celebrates various traditions, customs, and values.
