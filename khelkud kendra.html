Khelkud kendra

Welcome to the Sports Center Facility, operated by our non-governmental organization (NGO). Our mission is to promote physical activity, health, and community engagement through sports. We offer a state-of-the-art facility that caters to individuals of all ages and skill levels.

Our dedicated team of trainers and staff is committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment for everyone. Whether you're a professional athlete, a recreational sports enthusiast, or a beginner looking to get active, our sports center has something for you.

Facilities

Our sports center boasts a wide range of facilities designed to cater to various sports and activities. From well-maintained courts and fields to fully equipped gyms, we have the resources to support your fitness goals. Our facilities include:

  • Indoor and outdoor courts for basketball, tennis, and volleyball
  • A synthetic turf field for soccer, rugby, and other team sports
  • A fully equipped fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment
  • An Olympic-sized swimming pool with professional coaching
  • Specialized areas for yoga, martial arts, and group exercise classes

No matter your preferred sport or fitness activity, our sports center is equipped to meet your needs.

Events

We regularly host exciting events and tournaments for sports enthusiasts to participate in or spectate. From local championships to international competitions, there's always something happening at our sports center. Stay updated on the latest events by checking our website or subscribing to our newsletter.
