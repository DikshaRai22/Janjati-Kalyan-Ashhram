Frequently Asked Questions

What is Janjati Kalyan Ashram?

Janjati Kalyan Ashram is a non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare and upliftment of Janajati communities. We focus on various initiatives aimed at education, cultural preservation, and community development.

What programs does the Ashram offer?

We offer a range of programs including hostels,education support, sports and recreation, cultural events, self-help groups, and more. Our programs are designed to address the specific needs of Janajati communities.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers who share our vision. You can contact us through our website or visit our office to inquire about volunteer opportunities. Your skills and dedication can make a positive impact on our initiatives.

How can I support the Ashram's work?

You can support us by making a donation, participating in our fundraising events, or contributing to specific projects. Your support helps us continue our efforts to uplift Janajati communities.

Is the Ashram registered and recognized?

Yes, Janjati Kalyan Ashram is a registered non-profit organization. We have obtained recognition for our work from local authorities and governmental bodies.

How can I get more information?

Feel free to explore our website for detailed information about our programs, initiatives, and events. You can also contact us directly through the provided contact details for any specific inquiries.
