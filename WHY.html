Why We Started

Aama ko Maya Chhatrabas is a Hostel run by organization "Janjati kalyan Ashram, Nepal". It is non-governmental, not for profit,social welfare organization working among the indigenous tribes and the communities.

Objectives

Respected well-wishers and helping-hands!

We wish for your good health, prosperity and longevity. Janajati Kalyan Ashram, Nepal, since 2059 , has been running its service oriented projectsin different districts of the country for overall development of the Janajati (tribal) communities. Since the Janajatis have been dwelling in village and rural areas, who are economically backward, they have been deprived from the mainstream of development and their proper development has not been possible. For this reason, anti-nationalist powers have been misusing them. As a result, the severe problems like conversion, social animosity, communal conflict, and the like have been emerging.

Things To Do

Education

One of the primary focuses of the Ashram is to improve access to quality education for Janjati children.

Healthcare

Health is a fundamental aspect of well-being, and unfortunately, many Janjati people lack access to adequate healthcare facilities.

Livelihood Opportunities

Lack of job opportunities and economic resources often restrict the growth of the Janjati community.

Empowerment and Advocacy

Advocacy plays a vital role in ensuring that the rights and voices of the Janjati people are heard and respected.
